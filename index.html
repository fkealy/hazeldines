Our story

A dream, rebuilt
bolt by bolt.

01

The van

It started in 2024 with a tired old Iveco Daily, hand-converted by Megan & Julian Hazledine into a fully off-grid kitchen: solar-powered, stubborn, and parked where Cornwall runs out of land.

02

The journey

From the hatch came world-fusion plates that wandered, Argentine chimichurri to Swedish cardamom to Cornish sea salt, served to swimmers, surfers and anyone who followed the smell of coffee down to the cove.

03

The coffee house

Now the handbrake is on for good. You'll find us inside Psychopomp Micro-Distillery on St Michael's Hill, Bristol: same beans, more seats, sharing the room with gin stills and the Sonny Stores crew. Our own kitchen? Watch this space.
